Croatian Rhapsody: Maksim Mrvica Australian Debut 2020

Croatian pianist Maksim Mrvica is coming to Australia for a debut tour. A world renowned live performer, Maksim excites the crowd with electrifying energy and compelling emotions. Maksim started to play piano when he was nine and held his first concert by age 11. Despite harsh conditions during wartime Croatia, he later won first prize at the Rubinstein Piano Competition in Paris. Today, Maksim is the world’s biggest selling crossover pianist with albums sold over 4 million copies in 57 countries, winning numerous awards including MTV music award for the best debutant of the year in Asia. A ground-breaking pianist, Maksim’s live performance showcases classical music reimagined with electronic elements, combining strong personal style and rock star charisma. His best are Claudine and Croatian Rhapsody. His distinctive fashion flair also led to collaborations with world’s leading fashion brands.
