Maksim Mrvica Canadian Debut

Croatian born pianist Maksim Mrvica is for almost two decades the biggest selling crossover pianist in the world. His albums are sold in 57 countries around the world, reaching gold and platinum sales! Accompanied with his orchestra, Maksim will perform his very first concert in Canada.
